# Tonight's Pick — Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 30, 2026*

## Overview
Tonight's Pick is a personal movie collection app. Your data stays on your 
device. The developer does not collect, store, or sell any personal information.

## Data You Enter
Movie titles, formats, ratings, and notes you add are stored locally on your 
device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device 
unless you choose to enable Google Sheets sync.

## Google Sheets Sync (Optional)
If you connect your Google account, your collection metadata (movie titles, 
formats, ratings, and watch history) is synced to a Google Sheet in your own 
Google Drive. This data goes directly to your Google account — the developer 
has no access to it. Google's privacy policy applies to that data:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Camera
The app uses your camera to scan disc barcodes and cover art for movie 
identification. No images are stored beyond your device or transmitted anywhere.

## Third-Party Services
- **TMDB** (The Movie Database): Movie search queries are sent to TMDB to 
  retrieve metadata. No personal information is included.
  https://www.themoviedb.org/privacy-policy
- **UPCitemdb**: Barcode numbers are sent to look up disc titles. 
  No personal information is included.
